C++ language

C++ programming language was developed in 1980 by Bjarne Stroustrup at bell laboratories of AT&T (American Telephone & Telegraph), located in U.S.A. C++ was initially known as “C with classes, ” and was renamed C++ in 1983. C++ is shorthand for adding one to variety in programming; therefore C++ roughly means that “one higher than C.
